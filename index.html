ABOUT MESSY

Bringing chaos to the dancefloor since 2015. Known for eclectic sets that blur the lines between house, techno, and experimental electronic music. From underground warehouse parties to festival main stages, MESSY creates sonic journeys that push boundaries.
Based in New Orleans, MESSY draws inspiration from the city's rich musical heritage while pushing forward into uncharted electronic territories. Every set is a unique experience, weaving together unexpected genres and creating moments of pure dance floor magic.
